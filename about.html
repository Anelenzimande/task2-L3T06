About Me

My expertise spans across web development, programming languages, and full-stack application development. I am dedicated to creating applications that have a lasting impact and solve real-world problems.

In addition to my career in software engineering, I also pursue karting as a side activity. While my main focus remains on building software, karting provides me with a unique opportunity to challenge myself in a different field. I started karting in May, and I’ve rapidly progressed from rental karting to racing at a regional level. This dual interest helps me balance my professional and personal life, as both require dedication, discipline, and a continuous drive for improvement.

I believe that technology and sports intersect in powerful ways, and I draw inspiration from both to fuel my creativity. Whether I’m coding or racing, I’m always seeking ways to improve my skills, push my boundaries, and achieve excellence. My journey in both fields is a testament to the power of hard work, passion, and constant learning.

I am always open to networking, collaborating on exciting projects, and exploring new opportunities. Please feel free to reach out if you are interested in discussing software development, technology, or even motorsports.
